About this conversion

IndoPak (Indo-Pak) script differs from the Uthmani Mushaf script in two ways: letter & diacritic shapes (handled by the IndoPak font) and a handful of encoding conventions (handled by the rules above). This tool applies the safe, well-defined Unicode-level conversions and renders the result in an IndoPak typeface.

Note: a fully faithful Uthmani→IndoPak transformation (e.g. tanween idghaam display, all small-letter conventions) is font- and ruling-dependent. The toggles cover the standard reversible conversions; the rest is achieved through font rendering.
